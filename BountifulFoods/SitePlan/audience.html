Targeted Audience

Audience

The audience will be vegetarians, vegans, pescatarians, fruitarians, supermarkets, local grocery stores, and health-conscious individuals who value fresh fruits and freshly made fruit beverages. Our store provides organic fruits to those who prefer to limit their exposure to chemicals such as pesticides. Customer seeking food knowledge have the ability to speak to stores representatives to ask questions about how our food is grown, how to choose the freshest fruits, request recipe ideas, and the nutritional value of the food.
