Santa Monica Pier and Beach

Located 13.2 miles to the city center, the Santa Monica Pier contains one of the most legendary beach scenes in the United States. There you will find an abundance of restaurants, must see spots and bike paths. One such path is The Strand, which extends for 22 miles taking riders through Marina del Rey, Playa del Rey, and Venice, Manhattan, Hermosa, and Redondo beaches.
