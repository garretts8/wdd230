Purpose

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to advertise the availability of fresh, locally grown fruits, and to make known the nutrition value of our beverages. As Americans become more health conscious, the fruit and juice industry has grown, competing with flavored water. Our aim is to support healthy choices, by making locally grown, fresh fruits more readily available, and at an affordable cost.
